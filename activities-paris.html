Versailles

Planned
Place d'Armes, 78000 Versailles, France
Palace: 9:00am–6:30pm · Estate of Trianon: 12:00pm–6:30pm · Gardens: 7:00am–8:30pm · Park: 7:00am–8:30pm · Palace & Trianon closed Mondays and Dec 25, Jan 1, May 1
Light of Liberty: A 15-min VR experience marking the 250th anniversary of American Independence — meet Lafayette, Franklin & Washington and explore naval battles and diplomacy tied to the Revolution's connection with Versailles. Includes skip-the-line Palace access right after, plus the Estate of Trianon, Gardens, and temporary exhibitions.
Audio guides: 2 rented for the visit.
Palace ~€21 · Passport ticket (Palace + gardens + Trianon) ~€24–32 · Gardens free most days
Did you know? The Hall of Mirrors contains 357 mirrors. Mirrors were once so expensive that filling a room with them was itself a display of royal wealth and power.
