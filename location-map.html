Where we actually went

Trip Map

Upload a location export from Google or Apple and it'll show up here as your own colored trail, alongside everyone else's Check In pins from the home page.

How to get your export

Google (works on iPhone and Android): if Location History / Timeline is turned on in the Google Maps app, go to takeout.google.com, select only "Location History (Timeline)", and export it. You'll get a JSON file — upload that below.

Apple: Apple doesn't offer a simple built-in export of your location history the way Google does. The easiest path is to use the Google Maps app (see above, works fine on iPhone) or any GPX-exporting tracking app (many free hiking/running apps do this) — upload the resulting .gpx or .json file below.

Only points that fall within the trip dates (Aug 10–23, 2026) are kept — the rest of your location history stays on your device and is never uploaded.
