Today's plan

8:00 AM
Walk — St. James's Park (Guards Memorial to Buckingham Palace)
Route: start at the Guards Memorial · head into the park and pick up the lake's north bank path (better morning light, closer to the water than the Mall-side path) · pause at the Blue Bridge — the single best vantage point on the lake, with views both ways · continue west along the north bank toward Duck Island at the lake's west end, the park's main bird sanctuary and the best spot for pelicans, herons, and the resident waterfowl · exit the park at the west end and walk up to Buckingham Palace. Early morning (park opens 5am) means fewer crowds and more active birds — look for the famous pelicans, plus herons, cormorants, coots, moorhens, and tufted ducks along the way.
